How to make a sandwich!

These are the basic steps on sandwich making, by the end you'll have a delicious meal.

Here are the first few steps!

Step 1

You want to make sure you have 2 pieces of bread for each sandwich you want to make.

Step 2

Get out your condiment like Mustard and mayonnaise. Then your toppings for example; ham, cheese, lettuce, tomatoes, and whatever else you want to add.

Step 3

Combine your condiments on the outer alayer of the breat while all your toppings go into the middle to make the sandwich fuller.
